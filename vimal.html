VIMAL

Vimal is a masculine name of Indian origin, meaning "pure." It is a common name in India, sometimes as a surname and others as a given name. The most well-known is undoubtedly the mononymous actor Vimal, the star of the highly acclaimed film Pasanga in 2009. Vimal rose to prominence with his breakout role in the critically acclaimed Kanchivaram and has become one of the finest actors in the country India. The purity of Vimal and the purity of your little one will go hand in hand.
